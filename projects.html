Projects & impact

Where the work has actually gone.

Organizations we support often prefer to stay unnamed publicly. Below are anonymized summaries of completed and ongoing engagements. Named case studies and metrics will be added here as partner organizations approve them.

Data quality · Reporting

Cleaning up partner and program records for leadership decision-making

Helped a community-based nonprofit organize partner records, remove duplicate entries, and build reporting dashboards leadership now uses in monthly planning meetings.

Dashboards · Outcome tracking

Building a single view of program participation

Worked with a mission-driven education organization to consolidate participant attendance data scattered across multiple spreadsheets into one dashboard, reducing manual reporting time before each board meeting.

Donor records · Data cleanup

Reconciling donor records ahead of a year-end campaign

Supported a small nonprofit in correcting inconsistent donor entries and duplicate contacts ahead of a year-end fundraising push, improving the accuracy of acknowledgment letters and giving reports.

Training · Capacity building

Training staff to maintain a new tracking system

Trained program staff at a community organization to enter and maintain volunteer-tracking data independently after the initial system build, reducing reliance on outside support.
