Projects & impact

Real data work for mission-driven organizations.

Selected examples of nonprofit, education, healthcare, and community-focused data work. Some projects are summarized to protect organizational privacy, but the examples reflect real reporting, dashboard, data quality, and capacity-building work.

Nonprofit data systems · Affiliate engagement

Reactivating a dormant nonprofit partner network

Supported a nonprofit affiliate network of 2,800+ partner records by redesigning activation workflows, validating records, and improving reporting visibility. This work helped increase active affiliate participation from 11 to 98 partners in 3 months.

Data quality · Volunteer operations

Standardizing volunteer records across a global nonprofit team

Improved data reliability by standardizing collection processes and validation checks across 152 volunteer records in 10 countries, helping leadership maintain cleaner reporting and stronger operational visibility.

Dashboards · Leadership reporting

Building KPI reports for nonprofit decision-making

Developed dashboards and recurring reports to track affiliate engagement, outreach effectiveness, conversion rates, and processing timelines, giving leadership a clearer view of program performance and operational gaps.

Education data · Program reporting

Improving student engagement reporting at a university

Analyzed 300+ student engagement records and produced dashboards and recurring reports used by university leadership to monitor program activity, identify engagement trends, and support student-centered decision-making.

Healthcare systems · Process improvement

Improving reporting for healthcare licensing operations

Analyzed registration bottlenecks and improved reporting structures for a national healthcare licensing system serving 30,000+ professionals, supporting better service access, workflow visibility, and executive decision-making.

Data literacy · Youth development

Supporting student data literacy through DataJam

Coached and judged high school DataJam competitors, helping students turn research questions, datasets, and findings into clear analytical presentations.
