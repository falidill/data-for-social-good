Services

Practical support, not just strategy decks.

Every engagement is hands-on: we go into the actual spreadsheets, forms, and systems an organization already uses, and leave them more usable than we found them.

Data quality review

Find what's broken before it costs you a grant report.

We audit existing spreadsheets and systems for duplicates, inconsistent fields, and gaps — then provide a clear, prioritized cleanup plan.

Dashboard development

One place to see what's actually happening.

We build dashboards in tools your team already has access to, so leadership can check program and donor status without waiting on a report.

Donor & reporting cleanup

Records that match reality.

We reconcile donor and reporting records so giving history, contact info, and acknowledgments are accurate and consistent.

Program outcome tracking

Show the change you're actually making.

We help design simple, consistent ways to track program outcomes over time — built for staff to keep up, not abandon after month one.

Volunteer & participant tracking

Know who's involved, and how.

We set up lightweight systems to track volunteer hours, participant attendance, and engagement without extra admin burden.

Staff training

So the system outlives the project.

We train staff to maintain what we build — entering data consistently, reading dashboards, and troubleshooting common issues.

Reporting tool support

Excel · Airtable · Salesforce · Power BI

We work inside the tools your organization already pays for or knows, rather than pushing a new platform you'll have to relearn.

Data storytelling

Numbers a funder or board member will actually read.

We translate raw data into clear narratives and visuals for grant reports and leadership presentations.
