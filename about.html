About

A volunteer-led initiative, built by practitioners.

Data for Mission is a volunteer-led data initiative supporting nonprofit and mission-driven organizations with reporting, dashboards, and data quality — not (yet) a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

Founder

Led by Fali Honutse

Fali Honutse is a data science and operations professional with experience supporting nonprofit, education, healthcare, and mission-driven organizations through reporting systems, data quality improvement, dashboards, and operational analytics.

Data for Mission grew out of that work — a way to formalize and extend volunteer data support to more nonprofit and community organizations, alongside workshops run in partnership with Women in Data Science.
