Why this exists

Most nonprofit data lives in spreadsheets nobody trusts.

Small and mid-size nonprofits often run on a patchwork of spreadsheets, disconnected forms, and reports built under deadline pressure. The data exists — it's just hard to use. We help organizations clean it up, structure it, and turn it into something leadership, funders, and staff can actually act on.

01

Clean

Resolve duplicate records, inconsistent fields, and broken spreadsheets into a structure that holds up over time.

02

Connect

Build dashboards and reporting tools that pull from where your data actually lives — Excel, Airtable, Salesforce, or Power BI.

03

Decide

Turn numbers into a story your board, funders, and staff can use to make the next decision.
